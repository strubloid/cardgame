name: Stone Shaman
type: Earth
life: 4
shield: 0
attack: 1
mana_cost: 2
resonance: true
mana_generation_bonus_if_same_type: 1
tags: Resonance, Root

Description:
  - A mystical shaman who draws power from the earth, the Stone Shaman can root enemies in place,
	preventing them from gaining Shield and making them vulnerable to attacks.
Lore:
  - The Stone Shaman is said to commune with ancient earth spirits, channeling their strength to
  bind foes and protect allies alike.

rules:
  - Resonance: If you control another Earth card, +1 mana generation.
  - On play: apply Rooted to an enemy (Rooted: can't gain Shield).

synergy:
  - Sets up Quake Brute / Mud control lines.

status_reference:
  Burned: target takes 1 damage at start of its owner's turn
  Soaked: target has -1 Shield (min 0) while Soaked
  Rooted: target can't gain Shield while Rooted
  Aerial: attacker ignores 1 Shield when dealing damage

element_fusions:
  Steam: Burned + Soaked => next hit deals +1 damage
  Mud: Soaked + Rooted => target can't attack next turn
  Wildfire: Burned + Aerial => Burn spreads to another enemy if any
  Sandstorm: Rooted + Aerial => target Attack -1 next turn
